Second Chance NYC

From Incarceration to Independence

Every person deserves a chance to rebuild. We connect formerly incarcerated individuals with the resources they need to reclaim their lives, find meaningful employment, and build a stable future in New York City.

Breaking the Cycle

Incarceration doesn't have to be the end of your story. It can be a turning point. We believe in transformation through:

  • Employment - Access to job training and opportunities with employers who believe in second chances
  • Safe Housing - Shelter and housing resources to provide stability during your transition
  • Support - A community ready to help you succeed and stay on the right path

A Story of Change

"I spent 8 years incarcerated, and I thought my life was over. When I got out, I had nothing—no job, no home, no hope. But with the help of organizations and resources like these, I found stable housing, got a job I'm proud of, and rebuilt my relationship with my family. Today, I'm giving back by mentoring others on their second chance journey."

— Marcus T., Former Resident, NYC

Why This Matters

Without support, formerly incarcerated individuals face:

  • Employment discrimination despite rehabilitation
  • Difficulty accessing stable housing
  • Social stigma and isolation
  • High recidivism rates without proper support

But with opportunity, connection, and support? People thrive.
