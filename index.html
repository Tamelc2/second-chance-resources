Second Chance Resources: NYC

Your past does not define your future. Use these resources to rebuild, find a home, and start a career.

Employment & Job Training

  • The Fortune Society
    Employment services, digital literacy, and job placement for the justice-involved.
    Location: Long Island City & Harlem
  • Center for Employment Opportunities (CEO)
    Provides immediate paid transitional work and full-time job placement.
    Location: Manhattan
  • STRIVE New York
    6-week intensive career training and lifetime support.
    Location: East Harlem

Housing & Shelters

  • The Fortune Academy ("The Castle")
    Emergency and transitional housing specifically for formerly incarcerated individuals.
    Location: West Harlem
  • The Doe Fund (Ready, Willing & Able)
    Transitional housing combined with paid work and sobriety support.
    Location: Citywide
  • Exodus Transitional Community
    Reentry hotels and emergency housing with holistic support services.
    Location: East Harlem
